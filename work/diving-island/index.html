Diving Island

Adventure seeking divers and nature enthusiasts rise above the travel chaos to create an elegant, memorable brand.

Diving Island is a tour company headquartered in Reykjavik, Iceland, which offers personalized diving tours around Iceland, and specializes in using state-of-the-art diving equipment to secure the safety of its customers.

Challenge

The team wanted a clean brand and visual identity that would help it to stand out among the many tour operators in Iceland. We worked closely with them to design a strong brand identity, including logo, graphics, images, fonts, and colors.

We designed and developed their website, using Wordpress, and wrote taglines and web content. We also helped them to establish their editorial voice and tone for their website, ads, and social media.

We integrated the booking form with PayPal so that customers can easily book and pay for a diving tour directly through the website.

Our role

  • Branding and Identity
  • Visual and UI Design
  • Front-End Development
  • Web Development
  • Copywriting and Web Content

Results

The end result was a fresh web design that reflected the business' fun and adventurous brand, and with strategic content and keyword analysis, was optimized for SEO .

Customers could easily discover the business and could book and pay for a diving tour directly through the website.
